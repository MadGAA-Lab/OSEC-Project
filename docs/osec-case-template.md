[Topic name]

2. [Explain eligibility/process/steps]
3. [Answer policy/legal/safety reporting/follow-up]

**Time/Setting:**

* **Time:** [ ] minutes
* **Setting:** [ ]

### Example

**Background Information:**
A hospitalized patient continued febuxostat. She developed widespread rash and blisters; dermatology suspects SJS related to febuxostat. Febuxostat was stopped and symptoms improved. The physician calls the pharmacist about allopurinol genetic testing and drug injury relief application rules.

**Station Topic:**
Drug Injury Relief Application Consultation

**Tasks:**

1. Give ADR relatedness impression.
2. Explain drug injury relief application eligibility and steps.
3. Answer policy questions (time limit, who can apply, expected review time) and advise ADR reporting.

**Time/Setting:**

* **Time:** 8 minutes
* **Setting:** Clinical pharmacy office (phone consultation)

## V. SP Guide (Script)

### Template

**SP role:** [Physician/Patient/Caregiver]
**Emotion/tone:** [ ]
**Rules:**

* Follow the numbered script.
* If asked beyond script: “I’m not sure / I don’t know.”
* Do not hint.

**Numbered dialogue**

1. [Opening line]
2. [Case summary + first question]
3. [Follow-up policy/process question]
4. [Eligibility question]
5. [Candidate judgement question]
6. [Clarification question]
7. [How to obtain form/resource]
8. [Who can apply/authority]
9. [Time limit]
10. [When result available]
11. [ADR reporting / safety action]

### Example

**SP role:** Physician
**Emotion/tone:** Calm, concerned

**Numbered dialogue**

1. “Is this the clinical pharmacist? I’m Dr. ___.”
2. “Dermatology suspects SJS from febuxostat. I want to start allopurinol—do we need genetic testing?”
3. “Is that genetic test covered? Under what conditions?”
4. “What conditions must be met to apply for drug injury relief?”
5. “Based on this case, do you think the patient meets the conditions?”
6. “What counts as a serious ADR in this context?”
7. “How can I help the patient obtain the application form?”
8. “Can I apply on the patient’s behalf?”
9. “If we apply after discharge, is it too late? What’s the time limit?”
10. “How long does it take to know the result?”
11. “Do I need to report this as a hospital ADR?”
